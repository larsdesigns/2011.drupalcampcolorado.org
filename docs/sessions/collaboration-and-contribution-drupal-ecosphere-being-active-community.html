Collaboration and contribution in the Drupal ecosphere: Being active in the community

Session evaluation:
Evaluate this session
Speaker(s):
Ben Jeavons

It's not enough to just use Drupal, modules, themes, or support anymore. To excel your personal expertise and grow your business you have to be active and collaborate. Contributing back is the new marketing strategy.

So, how and where do we get started?

It doesn't have to be code either, you can contribute back in so many ways, let's talk about a lot of them, and how you can jump in.
