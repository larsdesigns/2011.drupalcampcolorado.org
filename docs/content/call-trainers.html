Call for trainers

Posted by Ben on March 31st, 2011

DrupalCamp Colorado 2011 will have space for pre-camp training or workshops on Friday, June 10th at the King's Center on AHEC (where the conference will be on Saturday and Sunday).

We're soliciting organizations that are interested in providing low-cost trainings or workshops to attendees.

We'll provide:

  • An all-day venue, with classrooms, WiFi, tables, chairs, projector, and screen
  • Lite breakfast and full lunch
  • Payment processing
  • Advertising your training or workshop
  • Classrooms hold under 50 people

We have space for 2 to 4 trainings/workshops but will not necessarily be choosing that many trainings.

The venue and food costs will be taken out of income from ticket sales, and profits will be split between DrupalCamp organizers and your organization to compensate the setup and support the Camp costs. The split is yet to be decided but will be quite fair.

Please fill out this form to let us know your interest in providing trainings. For questions, please use the contact form and choose "Trainings".

This form is now closed, trainers will be selected on or before the week of April 18th
