AppliedTrust

Howdy fellow Drupalistas,

We hope you're as excited for DrupalCamp Colorado as we are! If you're interested in performance, security, and availability, we'd love to have you join us at our Sunday morning session Keeping The Lights On - Operations and Monitoring Best Practices.

AppliedTrust has been helping organizations deploy and operate best-in-class infrastructure, security, and opensource since 2001. Located in beautiful Boulder, Colorado, we serve a variety of clients including The Children's Hospital of Denver, the City of Boulder, Jefferson County Libraries, Churchill Downs, Qwest, Vail, and Rally Software. We bring diverse infrastructure skills to the table, from networking and storage to Linux and Windows to app platforms such as (of course) Drupal. If you see one of the AppliedTrust Seals wandering around the Camp, grab us and say "hi"!
